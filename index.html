Sidharth Shukla

Indian Actor

Here's a time line of Sidharth's life

Early life
  • 12 Dec 1980: Sidharth Shukla was born into a Hindu Brahmin family in Bombay (present-day Mumbai) to Ashok Shukla, a civil engineer employed at the Reserve Bank of India and Rita Shukla, a homemaker.
  • 1980-2007: Shukla attended St. Xavier's High School, Fort, Mumbai and holds a bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Rachana Sansad School of Interior Design.Shukla has described himself as a very athletic child, and represented his school in tennis and football. He played against Italian football club, AC Milan’s under-19 team, on their Mumbai visit as part of Festa Italiana.
  • 2008: He started working in television series Babul Ka Aangann Chootey Na.
  • 2012: In 2012, Shukla appeared as District Collector Shivraj Shekhar, opposite Pratyusha Banerjee and Toral Rasputra in Balika Vadhu, later it become popular on Colors TV.
  • 2019: In 2019, he participated in the reality show Bigg Boss 13,[37] and was declared the winner in February 2020.
TV shows
  • 2008: Babul Ka Aangann Chootey Na was Sidharth’s first-ever lead role where he played the character Babul Ka Aangann Chootey Na..
  • 2012: Sidharth had joined the Balika Vadhu cast much later on but his presence in the serial had made quite the impact on fans and viewers. He was soon a favourite.
  • 2015: In Dil Se Dil Tak television soap, Sidharth played the role of Parth Ramnik Bhanushali. The series is loosely based on the 2001 Bollywood film Chori Chori Chupke Chupke
  • 2016: The winning streak continued as the late actor also bagged the first place in the reality show Khatron Ke Khiladi, the 7th season.
  • 2019: Sidharth emerged as the winner of the 13th season of Bigg Boss. His popularity after the show only doubled and his attractive personality was the talk of town
  • 2021: The ALTBalaji show Broken But Beautiful 3 was released on the 29th of May and the new season had marked Sidharth’s digital debut. Sidharth played the role of Agastya, a heartbroken aspiring director. He was featured opposite Sonia Rathee.It was his last show before his 😑 death.
Films
  • 2014: Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania marked Sidharth’s debut into Bollywood. He played the role of Angad Bedi, a supporting character.
  • 2016: In the film Biznes In Kazakhski
Awards
  • 2005: He won Gladrags Manhunt Contest in Worlds Best Model.
  • 2013: He won Gold Award in Best Popular Actor and ITA Award in GR8! Performer of the Year(Male) for Drama Series Balika Vadhu
  • 2014: He won Gold Award in field of Most Fit Actor(Male) for Balika Vadhu
  • 2015: He won Filmfare Award in Best Male Debut for Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania
  • 2017: He won Colors Golden Petal Award in the field of favourite jodi for Dil Se Dil Tak
Death
  • 2021: Shukla died on 2 September 2021 after suffering a heart attack at the age of 40. Shukla's family consisted of his mother and two sisters. His family informed the Mumbai Police, that Shukla was not under mental stress and they did not suspect any foul play in his death. His family also appealed his fans to not spread rumours about his death.
